# About

**Heading:** Operations, technology and people.

My experience ranges from operational leadership to technology implementation in sectors including R&D, facility management, security and smart mobility. I have worked on projects involving access control systems, AI camera technology and workflow automation while also managing operations, HR and teams through periods of significant change.

I help organisations build operational structures that work in practice. This means understanding both the technical and human side: the people developing the technology, the teams implementing it and the end users who have to work with it every day.

I learn quickly and adapt to unfamiliar environments — whether that is a new industry, a different organisation or a different country. I am motivated by complexity, by pressure and by building structure where it does not yet exist.
